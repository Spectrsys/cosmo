OSAF Server Bundle

The OSAF server bundle integrates several best-of-breed components into one easy-to-run calendar server package.

It pulls together Cosmo, the Tomcat servlet container, and many libraries into a single pre-configured application server.

What is Cosmo?

Cosmo is a calendar server.

With your favorite calendar program (Chandler, Apple iCal, Mozilla Sunbird, or any other WebDAV or CalDAV enabled client), you can share your calendar with other people by publishing it to Cosmo. Once your calendar is stored on the server, you can give permission for others to subscribe to it, and even modify it if their calendar client allows.
